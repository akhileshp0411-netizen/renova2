Renova

Turn Waste into Rewards — make recycling simple and rewarding

Renova is a network of smart Reverse Vending Machines that detect recyclable materials and reward users for responsible disposal. Earn credits, claim vouchers, and help keep our cities clean.

Our mission is to reduce litter and increase recycling by making the act of recycling rewarding and convenient for everyone. We partner with local businesses and civic bodies to deploy machines in high-traffic public spaces.

Quick overview

How it works: Insert accepted materials into a Renova machine. Sensors detect material and weight, credits are added to your account automatically.

Rewards: Redeem credits for vouchers, discounts, and cash transfers with partner brands.

Our Objective

Renova's mission is to reduce litter and increase recycling by making the act of recycling rewarding and convenient. We partner with local businesses and civic bodies to deploy machines in high-traffic public spaces, educate communities, and convert waste into value for users and partners.

Project goals

  • Reduce single-use waste in public spaces
  • Encourage repeat recycling behavior through rewards
  • Provide transparent tracking of collected materials
